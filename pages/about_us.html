About Us

In the second half of 2016 Cesco and Sjors, sim-racing aficionados, decided to use Codemasters' F1 2016 as a platform to engage in an online held competition. A few months later the Sunday's Raceday Online Championship F1 was born. The S.R.O.C. F1 proved to be an immediate success due to it's well organised foundation and it's enthousiastic members.

The S.R.O.C. F1 is all about funloving competition, unity, enthousiasm and developing your sim-racing skills. Since July 2017 the S.R.O.C. F1 has expanded it's reaches and has a twin-team called: "S.R.O.C. Racing". Both S.R.O.C. Racing & S.R.O.C. F1 are founded on the same principles and values: To bring sim-racers together..
